Circulating Supply Management

Community Rules & Quest Guidelines

Welcome to SYNTA. These rules exist to ensure fairness, transparency, and long-term sustainability of the SYNTA ecosystem. By participating in SYNTA quests, games, or community events, you agree to the rules outlined below.

1

General Principles

SYNTA is a community-driven GameFi project. Participation is voluntary. All rules apply equally to all participants. On-chain actions are considered the source of truth.
2

Quest Overview

SYNTA regularly hosts community quests including photo hunts, creative challenges, themed submissions. Each quest is announced publicly.
3

How to Enter a Quest

1. Acquire required SYNTA
2. Burn entry amount
3. Create original content
4. Submit with wallet & burn proof
4

Burn Rules

Burn amounts are fixed per quest. Burn transactions must be completed before deadline. Burned tokens permanently removed from circulation.
5

Submission Rules

One entry per wallet per quest. Content must be original and relevant. No stolen or plagiarized material.
6

Anti-Abuse & Fair Play

No Sybil behavior (multiple wallets). No reused submissions. No manipulation of voting or rewards.
7

Winner Selection & Rewards

Multiple winners per quest. Selection may be curated, randomized, or community-based. Rewards distributed transparently.
8

Rule Updates

Rules may evolve as ecosystem grows. Any material changes announced in advance. Active quests never affected retroactively.

Quest Flow Process

1

Acquire SYNTA

Purchase SYNTA tokens from Raydium or other supported DEXs to participate in quests.

2

Burn Entry Fee

Send required SYNTA amount to burn address. This is your entry ticket and creates deflationary pressure.

3

Create Content

Create original content based on quest theme. Follow all submission guidelines and rules.

4

Submit Entry

Submit your content with wallet address and burn transaction hash through official channels.

5

Win Rewards

Winners selected based on creativity, relevance, and community voting. Rewards distributed transparently.
